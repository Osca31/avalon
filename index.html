AVALON

Five piece acoustic guitar based band from West Cumbria.
Avalon were formed by Martin Kelly and Sam Lynch who started performing at family parties and gatherings. This led to them being asked to perform at their first public performance ,a charity concert at Rosehill Theatre.
After that they then asked John Thompson to join on keyboards and continued to expand their repertoire and experience.
Finally in early 2015 Alan Lowery on bass guitar and Alan Kewley on drums joined to complete the five piece band.
Avalon are a very versatile band as they dont play one style of music.Due to the acoustic elements of the band they bring fresh arrangements to all of the songs they perform.
Even though they are a five piece they have the ability to squeeze into small venues but still retain the feeling of a much larger band.
This makes them an ideal  for different sizes of venues and occasions,including pubs,clubs,parties,weddings,festivals and even a party in your own house !
